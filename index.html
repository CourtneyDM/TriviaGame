Trivia Game

Objective : You will be shown a sports logo, along with a question relative to that team. You have to guess the correct answer within the allowed time to score points.

Scoring : Each question is worth 10 points, with the chance of multiplying your points by answering before time expires.

For each correct answer, the remaining time will act as the multiplier - i.e. 6 seconds left will earn you 60 points.

Be careful - for each incorrect answer you will lose points based on the multiplier - i.e. 6 seconds left for an incorrect answer will cost you 60 points! Think carefully before you answer, and good luck!
